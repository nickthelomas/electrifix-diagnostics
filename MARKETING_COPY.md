## FAQ Additions

**Q: How is ElectriFix different from other repair shops?**

A: We've invested in professional diagnostic software that most repair shops don't have access to. This allows us to identify faults accurately in minutes, not hours. You get faster service, accurate quotes, and confidence that we're fixing the real problem.

**Q: Do you charge for diagnostics?**

A: We offer **free diagnostics** with any repair. If you choose not to proceed with the repair, a diagnostic fee may apply. Ask for details when you book.

**Q: Can you diagnose any e-scooter brand?**

A: We can diagnose most major brands and many generic Chinese scooters. Our system supports multiple communication protocols used by different manufacturers. Contact us to confirm compatibility with your specific model.

**Q: What if you can't find the fault?**

A: Our diagnostic success rate is extremely high. In the rare case we can't identify an issue, we'll discuss alternative testing methods or refer you to a specialist. We never charge for diagnostics we can't complete.
